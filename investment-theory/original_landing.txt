# Landing Page: An Introduction to Investment Theory

By William N. Goetzmann, Edwin J. Beinecke Professor of Finance, Yale School of Management

## Description

This is a hypertext textbook introducing investment decision-making foundations, covering portfolio theory, risk-return tradeoffs, diversification, modern asset pricing models, and capital market efficiency. The material is designed for a four-week master's-level finance module.

## Book Structure

The textbook comprises eight chapters plus summary materials:

- Chapter I: Capital Markets and Investment Performance
- Chapter II: Efficient Frontier basics
- Chapter III: Investor preferences and choice
- Chapter IV: CAPM and portfolio risk approach
- Chapter V: Security Market Line concepts
- Chapter VI: Arbitrage Pricing Theory
- Chapter VII: Beta calculations and discount rates
- Chapter VIII: Information and capital market efficiency
